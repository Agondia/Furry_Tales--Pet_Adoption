Our Success Stories

Pepper

Pepper, a sleek black cat, spent weeks at the shelter. Sarah, a social media whiz, saw his potential. She adopted him and started "@MidnightMusingsWithPepper," showcasing his naps, loafing, and "study sessions." Pepper's charm and Sarah's wit won them a following. Soon, "#MidnightMusings" was a student's good luck charm!From overlooked shelter cat to social media star, Pepper's purrfect journey proves black cats are just as lucky!
